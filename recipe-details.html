Yammi Chocolate Cake

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Vanila
  • Oil
  • Chocolate
  • Baking soda
  • Coocoa powder
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F and grease and flour two nine-inch round pans.
  2. in a large bowl,stir together the dry ingredients.Add milk,eggs,oil,flour and vanilla and mix well.
  3. Stir in the boiling water and divide the batter between the prepared pans.
  4. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  5. Cool the cake completely before frosting.
